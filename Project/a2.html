Tell Me Your Favorite Application

June 30, 2019

My Favorite Application is Lightroom CC. Lightroom CC is the mobile version of the Adobe Photoshop Lightroom. I like this application because it was not just a photo editing application, it has the function of a typical photo editor application but the difference is that the presets the you can use and reuse. I say reuse because the mixtures that you had created or applied to your recent picture can be reuse because it has the feature of "create preset" wherein you can save the mixtures that you had done and apply it to your other pictures.

Lightroom CC has a facebook group wherein the members are mostly from the Philippines. Many members in this facebook group are frequently sharing presets that they had done and the members can download it and apply it to their own photos.
