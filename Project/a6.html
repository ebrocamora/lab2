What is a Persona?
According to Interaction Design Foundation, personas are fictional characters, which you create based upon your research in order to represent the different user types that might use your service, product, site, or brand in a similar way. These fictional characters will help us on how we will design our project. We can use them to analyze the needs, experiences, goals and behaviors of our target users.

Here are the following Personas that we created using Hubspot:

1. Cindy N. Otime
Cindy N. Otime is a Senior Finance Associate in a big company that has 51-200 employees. Cindy is like the other people who is busy as bee. She reports to a CEO in the company. Her time is allotted to all work-related stuff and doesnâ€™t have time to do other things such as cleaning. Her preferred methods of communication are email, phone, and text messaging.

2. Boogie Wonderland
Boogie is a young man who is just starting to live with himself. He is starting to be an independent young man. He also works in a very big company that has 201-500. As a young man, most of his time is spent in different activities because now that he is an independent young man, he can do whatever he wanted. Boogie is active in social media because he posts everything that is happening in his life.

3. Roy
Roy is a typical working guy. He works in a small company that has 1-10 employees. He is one of the greatest assets of the company that he is working for. Being one of the greatest assets of the company, Roy need to stay at work in order to maintain his very good performance in the company. Most of his time is spent in making profit for the company he is working for. He has a lot of preferred method of communication because he is the one who is contacting the possible clients.
