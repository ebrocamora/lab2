First Week of USERDES Class

July 1, 2019

Last week was lit because it was the first week of meeting miss Jen wherein, I thought that miss Jen was like the other professors who is so strict. But when she introduces herself, the tone or the way she speaks is just like she was talking to her friends that has the same age as hers. I like that way she approaches and vice versa. In terms of the subject, at first, I was surprised because USERDES class is about designing an application for the users benefit but the first thing that she made us do is to write about ourselves and make an introduction.

That write up was followed by a write up again about our favorite application she even made us present it in front of the class. But miss Jen explained to us why she made us write and the only thing that I get about what she said is that writing is important in designing whether an application or website because the content is more important than the design, yes, you have the most beautiful, attractive, and amazing website but the content are nonsense or the content was not prioritize because of the design. I hope that throughout the term, I will learn or understand what does the user needs in a certain application and website. I also expect that miss Jen will stay the same as she is because it makes confident to speak and answer her question.
